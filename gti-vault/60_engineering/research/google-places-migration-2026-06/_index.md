# Google Places migration research

Implementation-focused research for replacing Foursquare as the primary PlacesProxy upstream with Google Places API (New).

## Scope

This bundle assumes the product decision is mostly made and does not compare Google vs Foursquare capability breadth. It answers: what must change in code, schema, operations, billing posture, cache policy, and UI/legal attribution to make the substitution.
